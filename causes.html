Gun Control

More information on Guns and Gun Control

Gun control focuses on whether or not people should be allowed guns, and how lenient gun control should be. Protests have been made on college campuses around the US due to open-carry laws being implemented, and the argument of the Second Amendment is often brought up in these cases. Many arguments are based on how strict laws should be to obtain guns, including the concept of requiring criminal background checks and psychiatric evaluations.

Immigration Rights

Information on Immigration Rights!

Immigration issues are on the process to become a citizen, deportations, and how immigrants affect the US economy. The argument can go both ways, with some people arguing that immigrants hurt the economy while other state they keep jobs and provide services.
